Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation is the art of transforming ideas into impactful solutions that redefine possibilities.

Sustainability

Sustainability is about balancing growth with care for people, planet, and future generations.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure forms the foundation for sustainable development and economic growth.

Empowerment

Empowerment is enabling individuals to realize their potential and take control of their future.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for
all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly
future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global
competitiveness.
